react-aria-tabpanel demo

Interactions to try

  • With focus on the active tab, hit Left, Right, Up, and Down Arrows to move through the tabs.
  • With focus on the active tab, (in some demos) type letter keys to navigate across tabs. (Not all demos because this feature is optional.)
  • Tab through the page. Notice that only the active tab receives focus.
  • With focus inside the active tab panel (on one of those links), Control+Up Arrow may put focus on the active tab (depending on whether your OS controls that combination).

These interactions are intended to fulfill the WAI-ARIA Tab Panel Design Pattern.
